EHDEN HMB

Welcome the EHDEN Heavy-menstrual Bleeding Study! This study aims to describe patient characteristics and treatment pathways of women diagnosed with HMB in real-world data sources that have been standardized to the OMOP CDM and are a part of EHDEN.
